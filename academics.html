Our Curriculum

A Rigorous & Balanced Program

Our curriculum is designed to guide students from early exploration to advanced specialization, preparing them for college admissions and global opportunities.

Primary Curriculum

Grades 1–5 focus on fundamental cognitive and social skills. We utilize inquiry-based learning to build active curiosity.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Phonics & Foundational Reading
  • Hands-on Mathematical Activities
  • Elementary Science & Nature Walks
  • Visual Arts & Introductory Music

Middle School Curriculum

Grades 6–8 bridge structured guidance and independent research. We focus on critical analysis and collaborative school work.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Algebra, Geometry & Computing Core
  • Applied Physics, Chemistry & Biology
  • World History & Literary Analysis
  • Dual Language Electives (Spanish/French)

Secondary School Curriculum

Grades 9–10 focus on specialized subjects, academic foundations, and deep preparation for secondary board examinations.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Advanced Algebra, Trigonometry & Stats
  • Physics, Chemistry, and Biology Core
  • Civics, Economics, & Geography
  • Board Preparation & Mock Series

Life at ABC

Beyond the Classroom

Student life is vibrant, collaborative, and designed to foster deep relationships, diverse hobbies, and community-centric values.

Clubs & Societies

From the Coding & Tech Guild to Creative Writing, Model UN, and Chess Club, there are 20+ student-led societies to spark fresh interests.

Championship Sports

Our teams compete at state and regional levels in soccer, basketball, track & field, and swimming, promoting collaboration and physical fitness.

Music & Fine Arts

Students showcase creative productions through theatrical plays, regular orchestral recitals, gallery exhibitions, and annual arts festivals.

Community & Impact

We foster civic engagement through active green recycling programs, charity fundraising events, and regular mentoring sessions for local youth.
